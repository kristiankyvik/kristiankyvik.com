The many distractions in the quest for impact Mar 12, 2023 (Updated: Mar 15, 2023) on kristiankyvik.com

It is surprisingly easy to get distracted in the quest for impact. The beginners mindset one has the first weeks after starting a new role vanishes exceptionally quickly. It does not take very long before you you catch yourself optimising for the wrong thing. Or worst, not optimising for anything at all.

Some of the symptoms are:

  • Decisions on what to prioritise are taken based on who in the organisation complaints the loudest
  • Teams worry more about having “enough things to work on” versus ensuring they are “working on the right thing”
  • There is a over-fixation on the optics around initiatives and feature launches are prematurely judged as successes
  • Many more…
