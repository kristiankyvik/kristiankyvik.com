City activities
- Walk up the Akerselva, the river, up to Fomoto cafe or Hønse Lovisas house
- Walk around Ekebergpark
- Walk around one of the islands, Hovedøya
- Dropin at any of the saunas in the fjord (SALT, Fahrenheit)

Longer walks
- Walk to Vettakollen top, then down to Sognsvann lake, ~2 hours
- Walk from Sognsvann to Skjennungstua and back, get a waffle or boler, ~4 hours
